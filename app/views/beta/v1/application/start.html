Apply for new export health certificates

This service enables you to apply for export health certificates to export or move live animals and animal products from Great Britain to the EU and Nothern Ireland. The service will send the Export application to the EU TRACES system enabling your importer to clone it into a Common Health Entry Document.

Use this service to:

  • Apply for export health certificates for the EU and Northern Ireland.
  • Retrieve the TRACES EU Import reference number.
  • Get PDFs of export health certificates in EU languages.

It takes around 10 minutes to complete an application.
