Before you start

This is a digital certificate, you will not need to print out the certificate. You can download a QR code to scan at the border post.

Make sure you have details of your export. You will need to enter detailed information including:

  • the commodity group, weight and quantity of the goods you want to export
  • the addresses and approval numbers for the exporter, importer and certifier
  • the planned departure and arrival times and locations
  • the method of transport and border control post
